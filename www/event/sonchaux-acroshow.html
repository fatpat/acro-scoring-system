Sonchaux Acroshow

One of the favourite of many pilots! Sonchaux Acroshow is one of the best competitions to many pilots. This competition will be shared with both AWT + AWQ and it will be final of the Swiss Acro Tour pilots. Sonchaux is known for it's amazing air show, so it is an honor for the AWT to be part of this event. The energy of the crowd in this event is amazing so there is no doubt that this years edition will be another unforgetable one.
