Nest Games AWT 01 results

The first stop of the AWT2023 is a success! The first acro competition has become reality and all the pilots are super happy how it turned out. Everything went according to plan, the safety measures were respected and besides some minor issues, the livestream was also a success and the people were super happy to watch it.

Thanks to Air Et Aventure for organising the event. We hope we can come back for 2024. Congratulation to the pilots who managed to finish on the podium. A crazy tight battle between the top 2 with just 0.025 points difference. This shows the precision and level of the best pilots in the world and it pormises an exciting season!
