Kings of the Box AWT 02 results

Dutch pilot wins Acro World Cup for the first time in history

The second stop of the Acro World Tour happened last week and for the first time in history a pilot from the Netherlands wins the world cup! It all happened at the Kings of the Box in Allevard, France. Because of the new over ground format in the AWT, a first time acro competition was held in Allevard and all the pilots were super happy with the competition. The pilots were satisfied with the altitude and the place showed that it has potential to have more competitions in the future.

After 2 runs, the top 2 pilots of last competition didn't have their best moment. Both pilots had some mistakes and made them lose points which put them behind Egor Hardcore and Axel Coste who managed to perform their runs really well. But after run 3, Luke put down a clean run where Axel and Egor didn't perform well which made the pilots really nervous because this could shake things up in the ranking. Unexpectedly Luke's run performed so well that it pushed him to the top of the ranking and made him win his first ever world cup! At the same time also making him the first Dutch pilot to win a world cup in the Acro World Tour history.
